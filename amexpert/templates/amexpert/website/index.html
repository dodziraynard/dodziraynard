Who We Are

AmExpert is a community on the University of Ghana campus dedicated to working closely with students to close the gap between industry and academia, creating new opportunities for students to learn industry-valued skills. We provide students with valuable resources and training to ensure that they have everything they need to face any challenges head on, achieve their goals, and better serve their community.
